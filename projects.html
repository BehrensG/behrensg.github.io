ETH1HMSR-SMS

ETH1HMSR-SMS is a one-channel data acquisition card based on the HMSR-SMS chip. The communication with a PC is over Ethernet. The card supports LAN eXtensions for Instrumentation (LXI) and RAW TCP and UDP connections.

ETH1ISO224

ETH1ISO224 is a one-channel data acquisition card based on the ISO224 chip. The communication with a PC is over Ethernet. The card supports LAN eXtensions for Instrumentation (LXI) and RAW TCP and UDP connections.

ETH1IRCAM1

Ethernet interface for the Flir Lepton 3.5 and 3.1R thermal camera modules.
